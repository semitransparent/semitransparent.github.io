bwin.com odds API

Searching for and finding an API to poll betting odds from bwin.com by a man-in-the-middle on their official app

I was searching for interesting statistical data the other day and stumbled across online betting. I never really got into gambling at all and was surprised how popular it is. The market is littered with more or less shady offers. Anyhow none of these seems to be pretty chatty about their data or even has an official API. Therefore I tried to analyze several websites just to find most of those betting providers try to obfuscate their backends API calls as good as they can. A little disappointed I picked bwin.com (because they offer a hell of a lot of betting data) and started to analyze their mobile application for more insights in how their API works.
